## Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE)

**GKE** is a managed Kubernetes solution offered by Google Cloud.
In GKE, you don't have to create and deploy the Kubernetes clusters, they are provisioned "as a service".

**Follow this codelab - [Deploy, scale, and update your website with Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE).](https://codelabs.developers.google.com/codelabs/cloud-deploy-website-on-gke)**
